Revision

Prompts for Reorganizing

  1. Is your diagram nonlinear?
  2. Have you considered other groups, subgroups, and relationships?
  3. Are the groups and relationships structured so that they make sense to you intuitively?
  4. Do your arrows depict accurate cause and effect relationships?
  5. Have you organized your groups on the page so that your arrows don’t overlap?
  6. Are the groups and relationships personal to you? (i.e. not just copied from your textbook)
  7. Is this diagram organized in a manner that would help you easily teach the topic to another person?

Prompts for simplifying

  1. Is all the information in the diagram crucial?
  2. Are you able to identify which pieces of information are most important?
  3. Can you justify why certain pieces of information are more important than others?
  4. Have you considered bigger groups that could further generalize ideas that are less important?
  5. Have you created enough subgroups to make the information easily digestible?
  6. Did you use any creative way of emphasizing key ideas? I.e. thicker lines, bolded words, highlighting, illustrations, increasing size, different colors, etc.
  7. Will you be able to return to this diagram in the future and quickly understand the logic it portrays?
